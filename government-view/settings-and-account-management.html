AI Models

  • Revenue Forecasting Model: Predicts future government revenues using historical tax data and economic indicators.
  • Tax Compliance & Evasion Detection Model: Identifies patterns of tax evasion among taxpayers.
  • Budget Allocation Optimization Model: Suggests optimal budget allocations across sectors to maximize impact.
  • Debt Management and Optimization Model: Manages the government's debt portfolio and minimizes interest costs.
  • Public Sentiment Analysis Model: Analyzes public opinion on government policies using social media and survey data.
  • Fraud Detection Model: Detects fraudulent activities in public spending and tax collection.

Data Sources

  • Kenya Revenue Authority (KRA): Tax records, customs data, and taxpayer information.
  • Central Bank of Kenya (CBK): Economic indicators, interest rates, and foreign exchange data.
  • Kenyan National Bureau of Statistics (KNBS): GDP data, demographic data, and employment statistics.
  • Ministry of Finance and Planning: Historical budget allocations and fiscal policies.
  • International Monetary Fund (IMF) & World Bank: Economic reports, debt data, and global economic indicators.
  • Social Media Platforms & News Outlets: Public opinion and sentiment data.
  • Government Procurement Portal: Procurement records and contractor information.
