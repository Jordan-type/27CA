Actions Citizens Should Take to Ensure Proper Fund Allocation

  • Report Mismanagement: Citizens should report any signs of fund mismanagement to authorities and watchdog organizations.
  • Participate in Community Audits: Engage in local audits and monitoring activities to ensure funds are spent as intended.
  • Provide Feedback: Actively use platforms to provide feedback on government projects and spending.
  • Advocate for Transparency: Demand transparency in all stages of budgeting and expenditure by government bodies.
  • Support Prosecution: Back the prosecution of officials involved in corruption to deter future mismanagement.
